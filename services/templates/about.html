Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Excellence

Golden Crest Services was founded with a simple belief — every space deserves to be clean, healthy, and welcoming. Based in Kasarani, Nairobi, we started as a small residential cleaning team and have grown into a full-service cleaning company serving homes, offices, and commercial spaces across the city.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for reliability, attention to detail, and a genuine commitment to customer satisfaction. Our team is professionally trained, fully insured, and passionate about delivering results that exceed expectations every single time.
